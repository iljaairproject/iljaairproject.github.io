ILJA AIR PROJECT.

History.

People are a source of pollution. Any of our actions create pollution. Cities are centers of air pollution, such as cars, factories and offices. The whole struggle is trying to reduce new emissions, but does not reduce current pollution. Trees process carbon dioxide into oxygen and purify the air, but they are located outside the city, and dirty air constantly remains in the city. In addition to carbon dioxide, many different pollutants in the air are dust, pollen, germs, smoke and other gases. All this gradually settles on the leaves of trees, bushes and grass, reducing the amount of carbon dioxide which they can convert into oxygen. In the room we somehow try to clean the air with the help of air conditioners and filters, but when we go outside we breathe dirty air.
The air and carbon dioxide in it are transparent. But the air in cities becomes visible, like fog. Particles of pollution in the air begin to play the role of catalysts accelerating the process of creating harmful substances for humans. The dirtier the air, the worse the health, new diseases appear, diseases spread more quickly. Polluted air is heavier than usual, it collects below. Animals and children are the first to suffer from air pollution, as they are closer to the ground. Man and the animal breathing in dirty air, filters it through himself and all this muck gradually accumulates in the body causing illness. The city will always be a source of pollution, although the forest will purify the air, the air will not become cleaner in the city.

ILJA AIR PROJECT.

What do I suggest?

Installation of air purification. Modular, easy to maintain and repair. At each intersection in the city, install such a setting. Full modulation, you can change the number of filters needed depending on the location. For example, increase the number of smoke filters near the factory. Or increase the number of fans for a place where there is no wind. Works around the clock.
Will it be possible to install it ourselves?
Of course, if you want, you can buy it and install it on your site.
How much does it consume?
Installation consumes a little, no more than a conventional 100W bulb.
Prospects for the project.  
Today it is possible to produce modules that clean the air from:
1. dust, pollen and germs, 
2. odors and smoke, 
3. disinfectants. 
In the far, it will be possible to create modules that remove from the air:
1. toxic gases, 
2. excess carbon dioxide, 
3. and other filters. 
At the same time, nothing will need to be changed to use the new modules.

ILJA AIR PROJECT.

What is it like?

A cylinder with a diameter of 50 cm and a height of 200 cm, connected to electricity. On the side is the status indicator of filters. Red - the filter needs to be replaced. Green - everything is fine. Side door with easy-to-insert blocks: electronics modules and filters.
